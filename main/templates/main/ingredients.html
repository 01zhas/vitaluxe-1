Caffeine

Caffeine, a natural stimulant, enhances daily life by promoting alertness and reduced fatigue. Through adenosine receptor inhibition, it stimulates higher levels of brain activity. Caffeine also promotes the release of adrenaline, which enhances metabolism and physical performance. Such benefits are frequently used by athletes to improve attention, decrease perceived effort during training, and boost endurance.

Sodium

You don’t need less salt. Chances are, you need more. We included Sodium chloride in optimal doses to help restore a sense of vitality in your day-to-day and help you replenish what you lose through daily exertion. Sodium is a primary electrolyte as it is found in its highest concentrations outside of our cells. It’s concentration dictates how much water is pushed into cells from our blood and other extracellular liquids. In short, having adequate sodium in your system ensures the water you drink *actually* hydrates you.

Chloride

Chloride is used by the body to ensure the correct level of fluid balance. If you don’t have enough you can become dehydrated. It’s as simple as that.

Potassium

Both our cans and sachets have included ideal doses of potassium to support increased performance. We included potassium for its primary role in ensuring your cells are adequately hydrated and supporting nerve function in the body.

Magnesium

The magnesium in our formula plays a key role in preventing general tiredness, fatigue and overall combating that feeling of being ‘flat’. Magnesium also helps to support a healthy nervous system and metabolism while a deficiency in magnesium is often associated with sleepless nights and general fatigue.

Phosphate

Phosphate is essential for supporting healthy energy levels and regulating muscle function. If you don’t have the right levels of phosphate in your system, you’re cutting yourself down.

Calcium

We included calcium for its vital role in fluid distribution, with it being especially important to ensure that your skeletal system is functioning correctly. In short, lower levels of calcium can hinder normal bone regeneration and healing - causing brittleness and increasing the likelihood of injury from exercise.

Zinc

Zinc has been known to contribute to normal carbohydrate metabolism, normal cognitive functions and normal testosterone production. This makes it one of the key components of our formula for aiding recovery and supporting greater performance.
